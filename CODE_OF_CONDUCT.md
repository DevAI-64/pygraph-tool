# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be respectful, constructive and welcoming.

Everyone is expected to communicate with patience and professionalism.

## Expected behavior

Examples of expected behavior include:

- being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences,
- giving and receiving constructive feedback,
- focusing on what is best for the project,
- showing empathy toward other contributors,
- keeping discussions technical and useful.

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or intimidation,
- insulting or derogatory comments,
- personal attacks,
- trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior,
- publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block users who do not respect these expectations.
